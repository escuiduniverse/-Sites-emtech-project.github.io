Ten most Uncommonly Used Filipino Words

Have you ever wondered about unusual/uncommonly used Filipino words? Are you not intrigued on how many terms or Filipino words that we don't usually use in our everyday lives. Here are ten examples of uncommonly used Filipino words, their meanings, an how they are use in sentences.

Pahikamas

English Word: Last Farewell

Definition: To say your final goodbye or last farewell, you would use the noun "pakimakas." However, if you are parting or saying farewell, "pahimakas" can be used as an adjective too

Sentence: Naghanda ng magarbong salu-salo ang pamilya ni Ana para sa kanyang pahimakas.

Kalupi

English Word: Wallet

Definition: a bag for carrying miscellaneous articles while traveling.

Sentence:Wala na nga kong pera kahit tignan mo pa ang kalupi ko.

Duyog

English Word: Eclipse

Definition: The total or partial obscuring of one celestial body by another while traveling.

Sentence: Tumingin ka sa kalangitan at makikita mo ang nakakamamanghang kagandahan ng duyog.

Miktinig

English Word: Microphone

Definition: A device that captures audio by converting sound waves into an electrical signal.

Sentence: Nabitawan ko ang miktinig habang ako'y kumakanta dahil naramdaman ko ang matinding kaba.

Pantablay

English Word: Charger

Definition: A device for charging a battery or battery powered equipment.

Sentence: Pahiram naman ng pantablay mo, sira na kasi yung akin eh.

Tason

English Word: Bowl

Definition: A round dish or container typically used to prepare and serve food.

Sentence: Kumuha ka nga tason doon at ng maihain ko na ang niluto kong sinigang.

Sambat

English Word: Fork

Definition: An implement with two or more prongs used for lifting food to the mouth or holding it when cutting.

Sentence: Kumuha ka ng sambat dahil may dalang pansit ang iyong ate.

Alimusom

English Word: Scent

Definition: A distinctive smell, especially one that is pleasant.

Sentence: Hindi ko ilalaba ang aking damit upang hindi mawala ang dumikit niyang alimusom.

Pang-ulong hatinig

English Word: Earphones

Definition: An electrical device worn on the ear to receive radio or telephone communications or to listen to a radio, MP3 player, etc., without other people hearing.

Sentence: Gumamit ka ng pang-ulong hatinig upang mas marinig mo ang ganda ng musika.

Labaha

English Word: Razor

Definition: An instrument with a sharp blade or combination of blades, used to remove unwanted hair from the face or body.

Sentence: Hindi mawawala ang labaha sa gamit ng isang barbero.
